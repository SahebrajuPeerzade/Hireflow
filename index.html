The Problem

Generations of knowledge, systematically ignored.

India's smallholder farmers hold irreplaceable, hyper-local ecological intelligence — built across centuries of observation. Existing AI agricultural systems push standardised advice down to farmers. None of them listen up.

🗣️

Oral & Embodied Knowledge

Farmer expertise exists in voice, touch, and pattern — never digitised, invisible to AI models.

📡

Top-Down Advisories Fail

Only 11% of smallholders adopt existing agri-tech. The tools don't speak their language — literally.

⏳

Knowledge at Risk of Extinction

As farming populations age, centuries of embedded climate knowledge is lost with each generation.

The Solution

VaniAgri listens first.

A bidirectional AI platform built for zero-smartphone, multilingual, rural India. No app downloads. No literacy required. Just a voice.

01
🎙️

Farmer Speaks

Send a WhatsApp voice note or make a missed call. Describe what you see on your farm today — in your own language, your own words.

02
🧠

AI Synthesises

VaniAgri transcribes, extracts agronomic signals, and cross-references with satellite data, weather forecasts, and 500+ community logs from your district.

03
🌾

You Hear Back

A personalised 90-second audio advisory, in your dialect, citing your community's own observations. You earn KrishiPoints for every log.
